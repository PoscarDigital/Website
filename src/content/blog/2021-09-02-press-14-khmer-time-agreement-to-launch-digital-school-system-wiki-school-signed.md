The National Institute of Education (NIE) and POSCAR Digital Co., Ltd. have entered a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to launch a digital school system, so-called Wiki School.

The MoU signing ceremony took place virtually via Zoom under the presidency of Hang Chuon Naron, Minister of Education, Youth and Sports.

The initiative aims at promoting good governance and e-management at teacher training institutions and schools to further enhance Cambodia’s digital economy and society. Lim Nary – AKP
