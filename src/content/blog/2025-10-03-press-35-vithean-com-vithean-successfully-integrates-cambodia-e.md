Vithean Successfully Integrates Cambodia E-Invoicing System into Its Accounting Platform

Phnom Penh,– Poscar Digital Co., Ltd. proudly announces that its flagship accounting system,Vithean(www.vithean.com), has successfully integrated with the officialCambodia E-Invoicing System(www.e-invoice.gov.kh) under the Ministry of Economy and Finance. This milestone marks a major step forward in supporting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Cambodia to digitize financial operations with greater ease, compliance, and transparency.

With this integration, Vithean becomes one of the first Cambodian-developed accounting platforms fully connected to the national e-invoicing infrastructure. Businesses using Vithean can now issue, manage, and transmit invoices electronically in full compliance with the Ministry of Economy and Finance’s digital invoicing requirements.

Benefits for the Financial Sector

This integration not only simplifies compliance for SMEs but also contributes to building a stronger financial ecosystem in Cambodia. By adopting e-invoicing through Vithean, businesses help drive efficiency, reduce risks of error or fraud, and align with international best practices in digital finance.

Mr. Sok Pongsametrey, COO of Poscar Digital (Vithean), stated:

“At Vithean, our mission is to empower Cambodian SMEs with modern digital tools. By integrating with the Cambodia E-Invoicing System, we are enabling businesses to stay compliant while improving efficiency and transparency. This is more than a system upgrade—it is a step toward preparing Cambodian enterprises for the future of digital finance.”

About Vithean

Vitheanis a cloud-based accounting platform designed for SMEs in Cambodia and the region.
